Submission

Authors are invited to submit papers (up to 6 pages) in English (in Pdf or Word) that adhere to the ACM WORD or LATEX template. Papers must be unpublished and must not be submitted for publication elsewhere. All submissions will be blind reviewed by the Technical Program Committee on the basis of technical quality, relevance to conference topics of interest, originality, significance, and clarity.
